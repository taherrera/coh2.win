Pioneer Squad with Hazard Removal Package

Pioneers are basic combat troops whose primary tasks are building structures, creating or clearing battlefield obstacles, and attacking enemy defensive positions. Can be upgraded to clear mines and cut wire, or equipped with a flamethrower.
